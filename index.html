Our Philosophy

More Than a
Tennis Academy

Polonsky Tennis Academy was founded on a simple belief: that great coaching changes lives. We develop not just skilled players, but confident, disciplined athletes who carry what they learn on the court into everything they do.

Our team of USTA-certified coaches brings decades of combined experience at every level, from local parks programs to international competition.

USTA-Certified Coaching Staff — All instructors hold current national certification and undergo continuous professional development.

State-of-the-Art Facilities — Six lighted courts with both hard and clay surfaces, pro shop, and a dedicated fitness area.

Tournament Pathway — We prepare and accompany junior students through USTA and ITF regional tournaments across Florida and beyond.

College Recruitment Support — Guidance for high school athletes navigating the NCAA recruitment process.
